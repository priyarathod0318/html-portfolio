My top 3 movies of all time

Zindagi na milegi Dobara

About friendship and how 3 friends get together after a long time and their adventures in spain and how we all need to live in the moment.

The Hangover

About three friends who go to a bachlorette party to las vegas and how within 24 hours their lives have turned upside down. Like they've lost the groom, have someones' baby and much more!!

Anyone but you

This is an enemies to lovers trope where they're at a wedding and fake dating and its really fun to watch:)
